moroccan vegetables recipe

4 tomatoes
1 can of chickpeas
2 squashes
1/2 white cabbage
2 onions
2 garlic cloves
spices
olive oil

put onions and garlic in olive oil in a pot
when onions caramelize, add tomato sauce and fresh tomatoes, then cabbage and squash and spices.
leave on small fire for 2 hours
add chickpeas 5 mn before stopping
serve with couscous, quinoa or rice
